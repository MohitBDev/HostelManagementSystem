User story:
Sprint 1 - 
Registration, change password, forget password, support, Attendence, Request

Sprint 2 - 
Internet banking, request, Processing, Feedback

Sprint 3 -
Completed request, feedback, live tracking.

EPIC -
As as student i want to see avalibility of the rooms, so that I can do my registration.

Staff -
As a staff I want to see the rooms avalibility, so that student can make the registration.

Acceptance Criteria -
GIVEN: I am registered and logged into hostel management system software.

WHEN: I select the room, date and number of days, months I am going to stay.

THEN: I can see available rooms and then I can book and pay the fees.
